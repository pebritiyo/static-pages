We are a huge supporter of the Web Platform Docs community initiative and a key contributor with over 3,200 topics seeded from MSDN. We have allocated software engineers, test developers, and program managers to assist the W3C with the work ahead. Moving forward, we will continue to add content as we work with the W3C, other stewards, and the rest of the partners in this effort.

We have been working with the W3C for decades: holding a number of leadership positions and engaging with a variety of different W3C groups through many individual contributors. We are committed to working with all members for the development of new open standards and the improvement of existing standards with the W3C. We hope we can count on your help too.
